Mixing loop

Grundfos MIXIT is an all-in-one mixing loop with flexible offerings and features designed to accommodate every need in both heating and cooling systems.

MIXIT has a built-in actuator, controller and sensors, making designing, installing and operating mixing loops simple and easy.

Modular systems

Modular systems, often known as off-site builds or engineered to order, are designed, engineered and manufactured in a safe, controlled factory environment. As the construction sector is pressured towards increasing efficiency, there is a growing trend towards numerous applications being built off site.

The DELTA range has been developed specifically to offer various modularized standard solutions to the building services industry.

Pressure managers

A Grundfos pressure manager is essentially a pump pressure controller designed to control your pump. The pressure managers are available in two solutions:

basic flexibility and all-round control, ensuring that your pressure needs are always covered.
