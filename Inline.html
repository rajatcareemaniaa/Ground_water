Inline single stage pumps


In-line single-stage pumps are typically used in buildings for air-conditioning and heating applications. The pumps are equipped with standard motors and mechanical shaft seals. They are close-coupled, meaning that the pump and motor are two separate units, making them less sensitive to impurities in the pumped liquid

Application Data

• Air conditioning
• Industrial heating
• Hot water recirculation
• District cooling
• District heating
• Drinking water treatment
• Industrial cooling
• Desalination
• Water distribution

Key Features:

• Compact design with small footprint
• Wide range
• Service-friendly, top pull-up design
• Low energy consumption Options availbale for shaft, seals & impeller depending on liquid, temperature and pressure
• Twin-head versions with built-in alternation and standby function.
