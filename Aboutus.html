COMPANY SNAPSHOTS OVERVIEW

OVERVIEW

Since its inception five years ago, S B Enterprises has been redefining the distribution chain with its unparalleled excellence in product sourcing and customer service. Our business philosophy of "Times Change but Values Remain" is taken to heart when it comes to the distribution business as well. Customer requirements are constantly changing due to globalization and our distribution division has kept pace with these changing needs.

OUR PRINCIPALS

SB Enterprises today partners with leading companies for their distribution business. Our efficient distribution chain has allowed our principal companies to focus on their core competencies and trust us with delivering the supply chain activities of marketing and post sales services.

Multiple Product Offering:

S B Enterprises offers a one-stop solution consisting of multiple products. We offer a wide range of electrical, electronics/instrumentation and mechanical products that gives us the added advantage of looking at stake holder product line with a holistic perspective. The products that we offer are the basic lifeline requirements for the functioning of any industry. Each of our business units constantly feeds information to the other on new projects, expansions and other industry related information.

Trained Marketing and Service Team:

S B Enterprises strength is in its people. Our trained techno-commercial marketing executives handle the entire sales cycle from initial contact to technical suggestions to providing the stake holder with a timely quote. They have detailed knowledge of the products and its applications in various industries. Our engineers undergo training modules, both in-house and with our partner companies. As a result, our engineers are up to date with the latest advances in technologies and problem identification and repair procedures.

Thorough Market Coverage:

Our clients both in the private and public sectors are located across Eastern Region. With over 7 sales and service personnel, our multiple located offices ensure that our stake holders receive support at all times Some of the major verticals that we service include Power, Mining, Oil & Gas, Refineries, Steel, Metal, Cement, Chemicals & Petrochemicals, General Industries.

AFTER SALES SUPPORT

Our service to customers does not stop with mere product selling. Our strong relationship with our customers is built on trust and good will, which we have earned through the years through our after sales services. We have a dedicated team of service engineers, who have been trained by our principals.

CENTRALIZED WAREHOUSE

By having a centralized warehouse, we have ensured that we maintain a Inventory at all times, to service a wide variety of stake holders on all our product lines on Ex-Stock basis. Our centralized warehousing facility is over 1000 sq.ft. in area and is located in Jamshedpur. It offers our stake holders the benefits of being close with well-connected Road & Rail linkages. The warehouse is also equipped with computer system to track material movement, storage and dispatches. On behalf of its principal partners, S B Enterprises handles the entire operations and administrative requirements including compliance with all statutory registers and returns.
